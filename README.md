### Поняття `viewport`

1. `width`
1. `initial-scale`
1. `minimum-scale`
1. `maximum-scale`
1. `user-scalable`

1. `<link rel="stylesheet" media="(max-width: 992px)" href="example.css" />`
1. `@media (max-width: 992px) {}`
1. `@supports` [?](https://habrahabr.ru/post/178021/)

  - `not`

  - `all`

  - `screen`
1. Media Features
  - `width`
  - `height`
  - `orientation`
  - `resolution`
